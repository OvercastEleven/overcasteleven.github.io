AN ATTEMPT TO DO ANYTHING

The Invisible Chair Project is an attempt to do anything. This website will be home to news and other information regarding this project.

Based in the Unity Engine, the Invisible Chair Project is a highly experiemental multiplayer experience tasked with finding the best elements in every game. Players will play and test a variety of ideas, with the goal of finding what makes a gameplay experience actually fun. The best of these ideas will undergo a more fulfilled development as a separate experience, graduating from THE INVISIBLE CHAIR PROJECT
